Assignment 1: UN Data over Time

The data used for the following visualizations was taken from 3 locations on the official UN website ([1][2][3]) and was modified to focus on the top 10 countries with the largest economies. They are:
  1. Brazil
  2. Canada
  3. China
  4. France
  5. Germany
  6. India
  7. Italy
  8. Japan
  9. United Kingdom
  10. United States of America
These countries were compared with respect to their Gross Domestic Product (GDP), Population and Number of Unemployed People.

1) Gross Domestic Products (using iframe)

The following stacked column chart shows the GDP for the top 10 economies in the period 1970-2016. The chart is imported from this google doc where the data used to create it is also available as a pivot table. Fun Fact: These top 10 economies were worth 2.16 Trillion USD in 1970 and grew to 51 Trillion USD in 2016.
