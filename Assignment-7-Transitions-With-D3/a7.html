Assignment 7: Interactive charts

The data used for generating the bar chart shown below is taken from the UN website and was modified to focus on the top 10 countries with the largest economies. They are:
  1. Brazil
  2. Canada
  3. China
  4. France
  5. Germany
  6. India
  7. Italy
  8. Japan
  9. United Kingdom
  10. United States of America
These countries are compared with respect to their population in the year 2016. There are 5 buttons below the bar chart. These buttons allow the user to visualize the chart in 5 different ways:
  1. In ascending order of country name
  2. Show all 10 countries in the same order as the previous state
  3. Top 5 countries in terms of population in the same order as the previous state
  4. Bottom 5 countries in terms of population in the same order as the previous state
  5. Currently displayed countries in ascending order of country name
  6. Currently displayed countries in ascending order of country population
  7. Currently displayed countries in descending order of country population
